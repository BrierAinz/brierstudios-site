FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

What is BrierStudios?

BrierStudios is a creative technology studio that builds tools, agents, and art inspired by Norse mythology. We work across web development, AI agents, generative art, and visual design — all unified under the Yggdrasil framework (Nine Realms). Our visual identity is Lilith, a dark fantasy goddess.

What is the Yggdrasil CLI?

Yggdrasil CLI is a command-line tool that organizes projects into Nine Realms (Asgard, Vanaheim, Alfheim, Svartalfheim, Muspelheim, Niflheim, Helheim, Jotunheim, Midgard). Each realm has dedicated commands for its domain. v2.5 supports slash commands and realm switching.

Who is Lilith?

Lilith is the visual identity of BrierStudios — a dark fantasy goddess with bronze horns, honey-gold eyes (#c8a23e), and a forge-iron crown inscribed with Elder Futhark runes. She represents the studio's visual language across all products. Current version: v5.1 with 9 realm variants.

Do you take commissions?

Yes. Custom Lilith variants, brand identity work, and Norse-themed web projects. Reach out via the contact form to discuss scope, timeline, and rates.

How can I support BrierStudios?

One-time donations via Stripe, monthly patronage via Patreon, or commission work. See the Support page for options. Every contribution funds servers, domains, AI compute, and the time it takes to build the Nine Realms.

Is the source code open?

Some projects are open source on GitHub. Check the Roadmap for what's public. The Yggdrasil CLI core is MIT-licensed; the Lilith character and brand assets are proprietary.

What AI tools do you use?

Custom LoRAs trained on PixAI DiT.2 (Tsubaki.2). Captioning is natural language (30+ chars), not Danbooru tags. We use ComfyUI workflows for batch generation and pydantic-ai for agent orchestration (Lilith Swarm v5).

How do I contact you for collaboration?

Use the contact form on the main site. Include a brief description of what you want to build, your timeline, and any reference material. Response time is usually within 48 hours.
